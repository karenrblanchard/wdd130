Meet Our Team!

Blanchard Hospitality Consulting was established by Karen Blanchard in 2023. Karen worked in the hotel hospitality industry for 7 years including management. She was inspired by the revenue consultant that she worked directly with while being a manager of a small hotel. After graduating with a BS in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Brigham Young University Idaho she decided that wanted to fulfill her inspiration of hospitality consulting and becoming an entrepreneur. She decided to start her own consulting business which became BHC. Karen has a true passion for the hospitality industry and serving clients who have that same passion. She strives to provide her clients with the best quality service with loyalty and competitiveness in the market.
