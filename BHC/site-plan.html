Purpose

About Us

Blanchard Hospitality Consulting was established by Karen Blanchard in 2023. Karen worked in the hotel hospitality industry for 7 years including management. She was inspired by the revenue consultant that she worked directly with while being a manager of a small hotel. After graduating with a BS in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Brigham Young University Idaho she decided that wanted to fulfill her inspiration of hospitality consulting and becoming an entrepreneur. She decided to start her own consulting business which became BHC. Karen has a true passion for the hospitality industry and serving clients who have that same passion. She strives to provide her clients with the best quality service with loyalty and competitiveness in the market.

Services

Direct Revenue Advising

Improve profitability with weekly consultation meetings. We will discuss the past, current and future forecasts to establish competitive rates to bring your property success. We will also address any other operational concerns or questions that you may have. Our BHC staff stays up to date with state legal requirements and trainings

New Hotel Startup

The hotel industry is becoming more and more competitive, making it difficult for owners and managers to take their property to the level where it needs to be. Are you a new franchise owner? Not sure where to start with the logistics of your opening? We can guide you and your management step by step to make sure that your opening is seamless and enjoyable!

Software Training

At BHC we provide specialized training for front desk staff to learn the lastest programs in hotel computer software. We also train teams on other front desk procedures including cashiering. This can help your customer service agents be able to give your guests the best experience at your property. Trainings are in person led by one of our professional staff and are catered to each individual hotel based on type of software, size of hotel and staff, and calendar.
